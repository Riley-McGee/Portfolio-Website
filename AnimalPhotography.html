About Animal Photography
Animal photography captures the beauty, character, and personality of creatures in their environments. From the playful moments of pets to the intimate details that reveal animal behavior, these images celebrate the natural world's diversity. I enjoy photographing my own pets as well as wildlife encountered during travels and outdoor adventures.
