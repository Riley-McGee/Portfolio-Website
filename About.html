ABOUT ME

Introduction

Hello! I'm Riley McGee, an award-winning second-year Games Design student at Bournemouth University.
A childhood of exploring video games has lead me to study games design! Specifically, I enjoy level design and would love to persue a career following this. I have experimented creating levels and environments within Unreal Engine 5 and Unity.
My game design journey began almost 10 years ago, starting with simple projects on Scratch during my free time at school. Over the years, my passion for game design has only grown stronger, leading me to pursue formal education in this field.
In addition to my academics, I have explored the area of music production, using software such as FL Studio, Audacity and GarageBand to create small projects with friends.
